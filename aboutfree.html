HATE US

Why all the hate? Love and hate are two sides of the same coin, so in reality, we actually love the real estate exam. It's a play on words, of course, and highlights the anxiety, the pain, the tension in your throat and chest that you feel when you sit down to study. Embrace the pain! It's the first step in starting your study journey and likely the most important.

Working for half a decade for some of the most well-known eLearning and prep websites out there, we learned a lot about the industry! And we hate a lot of what we learned :)

"Big Prep" uses a lot of tricky and manipulative marketing techniques while not putting much effort into updating or improving their content. We hate try-hard marketing and prefer being accurate, competent, sincere, helpful, fun, and straightforward. We have a huge drive to make a helpful product, and this drive will never waver, only improve over time! So come along for the ride, reach out if you have any questions about who we are, and study well!
